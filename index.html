About Us

The license that lets us do the job right — not the workaround.

Most contractors are limited to cosmetic work and have to bring in separate licensed subs the moment a project touches structural, electrical, or plumbing systems. We hold an unlimited general contractor license, which means we manage those pieces directly — no markup chain, no coordination gaps, no waiting on the next guy to show up.

We're focused on residential renovation and new construction across the Charlotte metro, with commercial GC capacity coming as we grow.
